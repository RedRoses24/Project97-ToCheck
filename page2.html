What is Sign Language & Signdo?
×

Sign language is a natural and visual form of language that uses movements and expression to convey meaning between people. There are 3 types of Sign-language:American Sign Language (ASL), British Sign Language (BSL) and Australian Sign Language (Auslan). Signdo helps make Sign language easy and fun with videos and tips!
